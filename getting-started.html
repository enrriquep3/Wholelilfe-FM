Getting Started

Your first step toward
root-cause care.

Beginning care at WholeLife Functional Medicine is designed to be simple, clear, and personalized. From your first intake to your follow-up plan, each step is built to help uncover root causes and support your health journey.

Start today by filling out the contact form to connect with us and take the next step toward a more thoughtful approach to health and healing.

Start Intake View Pricing

What to Expect

A thoughtful, personalized process designed to look deeper and build a plan that fits your life.

A root-cause based approach to illness and dysfunction

No more simply treating symptoms. Let’s work together to figure out the causes of the issues you are suffering from.

Lifestyle before medication

The conventional health care model likes to start with medication. WholeLife focuses on lifestyle modifications first whenever possible, especially when a root cause has been uncovered.

A Biblical-based approach to life, health, and well-being

At WholeLife, spiritual health is considered foundational and central to overall well-being. Practices such as gratitude, faith, joy, prayer, and service may be meaningful parts of your health journey.

Ongoing support and guidance

This is not meant to be a one-time visit. Membership care is designed to provide continued support, provider portal communication, and guidance along the way.

How to Get Started

A simple step-by-step process so you know what happens next.

1

Start by filling the Contact Form

Use the contact form to make your first connection with us and we’ll respond with next steps.

2

Complete your intake

Share your health history, current concerns, and goals so your care can be tailored to you.

3

Initial intake appointment

Your first visit includes a thorough review of your history and current medical concerns, along with initial nutrition and lab recommendations.

4

Testing and evaluation

Targeted lab recommendations may be made based on your case to help uncover deeper imbalances and guide next steps.

5

Follow-up and treatment plan

At your follow-up visit, results are reviewed and a personalized treatment plan and recommendations are started.

6

Ongoing membership support

Continue with the membership plan that best fits your needs for follow-up visits, support, and ongoing guidance.

What Your First Visits Include

Your care begins with a structured two-visit start designed to build clarity and direction.

Visit 1 — Intake appointment

60 minutes

Includes a thorough review of patient history and current medical concerns, plus initial nutrition and lab recommendations.

Labs are not included in the visit cost.

Visit 2 — Follow-up appointment

30–60 minutes

Includes review of results and initiation of a tailored treatment plan and recommendations.

Virtual care in Ohio

At this time, WholeLife is offering virtual assessments for those located in the state of Ohio, with the long-term goal of a physical location in the future.

Who Functional Medicine Is For

Functional medicine is ideal for patients who need deeper answers, better support, or a proactive approach to long-term wellness.

Patients with symptoms no one else has been able to figure out

For those looking for a deeper investigation into ongoing or unexplained health concerns.

Patients with chronic disease looking for better support

For those who want a more personalized and root-cause–focused plan alongside their current care.

Healthy patients wanting to stay healthy and be proactive

For those who want a more complete picture of their health and practical guidance for prevention and optimization.

How to Prepare

A few simple steps can help you get the most out of your first visit.

Complete your intake forms

Use the contact formto make your first connection with us and we’ll respond with next steps.

Gather prior records

Bring any recent lab work, test results, or relevant medical records if available.

List medications and supplements

Make note of what you currently take so your recommendations can be more accurate and personalized.

Write down your main concerns

List your main symptoms, concerns, and health goals so nothing important gets missed.

Bring notes

Have a notebook or notes app ready so you can keep track of recommendations and next steps.

Come ready with questions

An open mind and thoughtful questions can help make your visit even more productive.
