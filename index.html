What Is Functional Medicine?

Looking beyond symptoms to understand the whole person.

Functional medicine is a root-cause–based approach that looks beyond symptoms to understand the whole person. We, at WholeLife Functional Medicine, create personalized plans that may include a combination of advanced testing, lifestyle changes, and integrative therapies.

The goal is simple: help you achieve optimal health and your highest quality of life.

What to Expect at WholeLife

A thoughtful approach to illness, healing, and long-term health.

A root-cause based approach to illness and dysfunction

No more simply treating symptoms. Let’s work together to figure out the causes of the issues you are suffering from.

Lifestyle before medication

The conventional health care model likes to start with medication. Let’s flip the script back to what it used to be and what it should be: lifestyle modifications first. Many chronic diseases and recurrent illnesses can be alleviated by changing a few simple things, especially once a root-cause has been discovered.

A Biblical-based approach to life, health, and well-being

At WholeLife, you will be getting a biblically-based approach. We believe spiritual health is foundational and central to our overall health and wellbeing. Things like forgiveness, serving, gratitude, faith, joy, prayer and even fasting all are important factors to our overall well-being and may often be recommended for you on your health journey.

Why WholeLife

Care centered on the whole person.

The name “Wholelife” was born out of Romans 12:1 - “Therefore, I urge you, brothers and sisters, in view of God’s mercy, to offer your bodies as a living sacrifice, holy and pleasing to God—this is your true and proper worship.” The sad reality is that too often we don’t think of what we do with our bodies - the foods we eat, the frequency of exercise, or how we challenge our minds - as worship to God, but the Bible tells us that it is! So that’s what our goal is here at WholeLife.

We want to partner with what God is already doing in your life - physically, mentally, emotionally and spiritually - to help you live the life you were made to live in worship to Him.
