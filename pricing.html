The Essentials Plan

For those who want a deeper look through bloodwork without ongoing provider visits.

The Essentials Lab Panel

$350

Not necessarily needing a provider's help, but just looking for a more thorough evaluation through bloodwork into what’s going on in your body?

This curated bloodwork panel includes 50+ specific functional markers assessing various levels of cardiometabolic health, endocrine health, essential micronutrient levels, and inflammation levels.

Panel includes:

Complete blood counts, comprehensive metabolic panel (including renal function, liver function, potassium, sodium, etc.), lipids, TSH/T3/T4, Vitamin D, Vitamin B12, folate, Magnesium RBC, hsCRP, ANA, homocysteine, A1C, fasting insulin, Apolipoprotein B, Lipoprotein A, testosterone (men), and estradiol (women).

The “The Essentials” does not include a visit to review labs or discuss recommendations. Results are released to you to decide next steps. You may choose to follow up with WholeLife as a new patient or discuss them with another practitioner you are already established with.
