About WholeLife

Hello, I’m Zach- Welcome to WholeLife!

I am a husband to my wife, Carly, and father to my two boys. We love being outdoors, serving at our Church, and spending time with our awesome friends and family.

I have worked for the last 16 years in healthcare in the local area. Currently, I work full-time at a wonderful private practice as a Family Medicine Nurse Practitioner. I care for patients of all ages- newborn to elderly and every age in-between! Before I became a nurse practitioner I was also an RN in the emergency department and a pharmacy technician.

I am so grateful for the experiences that brought me to where I am today; although some of them have been more enjoyable than others. While I am a provider; I am also a patient. I have had Crohn’s Disease since I was 19 years old. I never knew that feeling “normal” again was even possible. For many years, I had fear of what that future looked like. BUT GOD! Through answered prayer, lifestyle changes, treatments, hard work, and lots of learning, I am thrilled to say I am fully in remission- something I never dreamed was possible. Trust me when I say, I want others to experience that for themselves as well.

So, if you would ever ask “why functional medicine?” - my simple answer would be, “the practical principles and root cause analysis has the capability to change a life, and I can testify to that!” I want other people to experience all that life has to offer them, and I can’t wait to help!

Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage individuals to steward their health with purpose, honor God through intentional living, and pursue lasting wellness through a root-cause, whole-person approach to care.
